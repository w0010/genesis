    001

    Mission

    To communicate, educate, and connect in the visual language.

    To distill symbolic meaning into visual abstractions, producing useful analogues of one’s emotive objectives.

    To make timeless design by challenging the trends and seeking the element of surprise.

    To attain visual power through collaborative effort towards an asthetic ideal.

    I live and work in Portland Maine, United States. I work from a background in traditional oil painting, figurative sculpture, and a bit of graphic design.
