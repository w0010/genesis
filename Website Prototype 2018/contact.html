    Considerations

    I have an array of question I usually fire at my new inquiries. I will ask them again in an organic conversation, but here's for considering:

    • There exists a spectrum between a technician and a hired creative. Neither one is better than the other (nor are they mutually exclusive), but a potential client probably knows which one they are looking for. This is one of the first topics we'll discuss.

    • I am fluid and adaptable to many needs, but I cannot be a jack-of-all trades (master of none). I work exclusively in black and grey dotwork and very limited color. Further: as much as I admire and enjoy flowers, it just isn't a strength of mine. If however you are looking for strange botanic/organic forms, please disregard this. :)

    That's about it for the most general preliminaries. Any more specific and I would need to have a topic, so send me an email! I love to discuss.
